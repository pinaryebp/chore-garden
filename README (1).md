# 🌱 Chore Garden — Pınar & Cris

A shared chore tracker where every task you complete plants something in your garden!
Watch your garden grow with 🌱🌿🌷🌻🌳 as you keep your home tidy together.

## Features

- **📋 To Do list** — See what needs doing, with auto-reset timers (daily, every 2 days, weekly, etc.)
- **🌱 Quick logging** — One tap to log who did what
- **🌻 Garden scoreboard** — Weekly scores with blooming garden visualization
- **📊 Growth charts** — Track your progress week by week and month by month
- **⚙️ Customizable chores** — Add, edit, delete chores with custom emojis and difficulty

## How to Deploy (Free, No Coding Needed!)

### Step 1: Create a GitHub account
1. Go to **github.com** → **Sign Up**
2. Just need an email + password

### Step 2: Create a new repository
1. Click the **+** button (top right) → **New repository**
2. Name it: **chore-garden**
3. Select **Public**
4. Check ✅ **Add a README file**
5. Click **Create repository**

### Step 3: Upload the app files
1. In your repository, click **Add file** → **Upload files**
2. Drag and drop these 2 files:
   - `app.py`
   - `requirements.txt`
3. Click **Commit changes**

### Step 4: Deploy on Streamlit Cloud
1. Go to **share.streamlit.io**
2. Click **Sign in with GitHub**
3. Click **New app**
4. Select:
   - **Repository:** your-username/chore-garden
   - **Branch:** main
   - **Main file path:** app.py
5. Click **Deploy!**
6. Wait 2-3 minutes for it to build

### Step 5: Use it on your phones!
- You'll get a link like: `https://chore-garden.streamlit.app`
- Open this link on both your phones
- **Bookmark it** or **add to Home Screen**

#### Add to Home Screen:
- **Android:** Open in Chrome → tap ⋮ → "Add to Home Screen"
- **iPhone:** Open in Safari → tap Share → "Add to Home Screen"

## Auto-Reset Schedule

Chores automatically uncheck themselves based on their schedule:
- **Every day** — Resets 24 hours after completion (cooking, dishes, bed making)
- **Every 2 days** — Resets after 48 hours (trash)
- **Every 3 days** — Resets after 72 hours (laundry)
- **Every week** — Resets after 7 days (vacuuming, bathroom)
- **No repeat** — Stays checked once done (organize closet)

You can change any chore's schedule in the ⚙️ Chores tab.

## Troubleshooting

- **App looks weird?** Try refreshing the page
- **Data not saving?** Make sure you're using the same URL on both phones
- **Want to start fresh?** Use the Reset button in ⚙️ Chores tab
